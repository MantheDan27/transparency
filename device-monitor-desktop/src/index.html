How Transparency Works

        
Transparency scans your local network only. All scan data stays on your machine unless you explicitly use the Enrich feature.


        
What stays local

        
    
          
  • Device inventory — all discovered device details, history, and tags
    • 
          
  • Scan history — port changes, IP changes, presence data
    • 
          
  • Alert rules and logs — stored locally in your app data folder
    • 
          
  • Snapshots — saved network states for comparison
    • 
        


        
What enrichment sends (opt-in)

        
    
          
  • network_metadata — Device IP address on your local network
    • 
          
  • device_identity — Device hostname (if DNS-resolvable)
    • 
          
  • service_inventory — List of open TCP ports detected by scanner
    • 
        

        
The enrichment service runs locally (localhost) — no data sent to the internet.


        
Per-probe disclosure

        
Deep scan uses TCP connect probes, banner grabbing, TLS certificate inspection, mDNS queries, SSDP M-SEARCH, and optional NetBIOS queries. All probes target devices on your own network.


        
Deletion controls

        
Use the Data Management section above or the Data Ledger tab for granular deletion controls. Deletions are permanent and logged.

      


      
      

        
Per-Device Deletion

        

          

            
Forget a Device

            
Removes a device's name, tags, history, and scan results from this app. Does not affect the device itself.

          

          

            
            
          

          

            
            
            
          

        


        


        

          

            
Delete All Cloud Data

            
Permanently removes all enrichment history. Logged in the Data Ledger.

          

          
        


        


        

          

            
Clear Local Scan Snapshot

            
Resets the baseline for drift detection. Next scan starts fresh.
